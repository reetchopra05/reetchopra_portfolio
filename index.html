About Me

I am an Economics student at Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies, driven by a curiosity that refuses to stay within textbook boundaries. My work sits at the intersection of economic theory, data science, and the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

From analysing how AI is reshaping identity economies to representing India on the world stage, I bring both analytical rigour and a genuine passion for understanding how systems — economic, social, technological — shape human behaviour.

I believe the most interesting questions live at the borders between disciplines. That's where I choose to work.
